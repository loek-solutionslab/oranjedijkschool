02 — what's technically wrong

● six categories of issues.

Each one is a small fix individually. Together they explain why the current site struggles in search and on mobile.

seo

core seo is missing

  • 9 of 10 pages have a too-short or generic title
  • no meta descriptions anywhere — google writes its own
  • 4 pages have no h1, 2 pages have multiple h1s
  • content redirects before being served (canonical chain)
social

no social previews

  • missing og:description on all 10 pages
  • missing og:image — shares look broken on facebook & whatsapp
  • twitter card requires an image to render
security

basic security headers absent

  • no content-security-policy header
  • no x-frame-options — site can be iframed/clickjacked
  • external links missing rel="noopener"
a11y

accessibility issues

  • 15 images without alt text
  • buttons without accessible names on every page
  • 16 focusable elements hidden inside aria-hidden
performance

heavy & slow

  • 2.1 mb per page — way above mobile budget
  • server response (ttfb): ~797ms
  • below-fold images not lazy-loaded
  • oversized images served on every device
content

content & links

  • skipped heading levels on 3 pages
  • thin content (under min word count) on 4 pages
  • 1 broken external link, 1 orphan page
  • duplicate url paths (e.g. /copy-of-…)

04 — top 5 priorities

● if you fix only five things, fix these.

  1. 01

    rewrite the homepage with an emotional hook

    Address what expat parents actually fear (language loss, returning later) and lead with an outcome, not a feature list.

  2. 02

    add parent testimonials and success stories

    A school is a high-trust purchase. Real names + real outcomes are the highest-leverage content you don't yet have.

  3. 03

    make enrollment prominent — with urgency

    Limited spots, sibling priority, two intake moments. These are real constraints — communicate them and conversions go up.

  4. 04

    replace technical info with benefit-led copy

    "Onder toezicht NL Onderwijsinspectie" is a feature; "Officially supervised — like a school in the Netherlands" is a benefit.

  5. 05

    put a clear cta on every page

    Right now most pages are dead-ends. Each page should answer: "if a parent is convinced now, what do they do next?"
